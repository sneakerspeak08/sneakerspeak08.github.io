Project Overview:

Application purpose: This application is designed to be a gallery and portfolio for a photographer.

Intended users: Intended users of this site are potential clients looking to book the photographer for a photoshoot or just browse the available images.

Overview of website content: Most of the content is automotive images taken at car meets and scenic views, with some images being senior portraits.

Page Design:

HOME PAGE:

The name of the page: Home

Purpose of the page: Introduce users to the photographer (Landon Draper).

Audience/Users of page: Potential Clients and gallery viewers.

What will be content of the page? Introducing users to the photographer and display a few images showcasing the photographer's work. Will also feature a small introduction (the bigger introduction will be in the about me page)

Will the page contain buttons or hyperlinks or drop downs? Yes, it will include social media hyperlinks.

What actions will happen in the page? When the user clicks on a social media link, it will take them to a separate web page.

ABOUT PAGE:

The name of the page: About me

Purpose of the page: Introduce Landon Draper, who he is, what he does, his social medias, etc.

Audience/Users of page: Potential customers.

What will be content of the page? This page will talk about who Landon Draper is, his history doing photography, what things he takes pictures of, and a little about his experience.

CONTACT PAGE:

The name of the page: Contact

Purpose of the page: A page to contact the photographer about questions or interests in a photoshoot.

Audience/Users of page: Potential customers.

What will be content of the page? The page will include a contact me form via email.

Is this a page where you are asking users to enter data on the page? Yes

Will the page contain buttons or hyperlinks or drop downs? Submit form button.

What actions will happen in the page? When the user fills out the form to contact Landon, it will then submit and be sent to his email.

FAQ PAGE:

The name of the page: FAQ

Purpose of the page: To answer common questions asked about doing a photoshoot with the photographer.

Audience/Users of page: Customers, Site viewers.

What will be content of the page? A dropdown menu containing questions and answers.

Will the page contain buttons or hyperlinks or drop downs? Contains drop downs for each question asked.

What actions will happen in the page? When the user clicks the dropdown for the question, it will contain the common answer.

PHOTO GALLERY:

The name of the page: Gallery

Purpose of the page: Display Landon's current work.

Audience/Users of page: Site viewers, clients.

What will be content of the page? Images taken by the photographer.

What actions will happen in the page? This page will be an interactive photo gallery where users can slide through all the images and click on one if interested where the image will then expand.

Dynamic Functionality:

GALLERY: Page: Gallery - Use: To provide potential clients an easy-to-view portfolio/gallery of current images that have been taken.

CONTACT FORM: Page: Contact - Use: For clients to contact about photo shoots.

FAQ DROPDOWN: Page: FAQ - Use: To give clients a list of common questions asked about doing a photoshoot.
